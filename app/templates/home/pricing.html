Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and features

Is the free version really free forever?

Yes! Our freemium plan is completely free with no time limits. You can generate up to 30 bills per month and manage up to 20 inventory items without any cost.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to premium anytime to unlock unlimited features. You can also downgrade back to the free plan if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets for premium subscriptions.

Is my data secure and backed up?

Yes, your data is completely secure with automatic cloud backups. We use enterprise-grade security measures to protect your business information.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! Free users get email support, while premium users enjoy priority support with faster response times and dedicated assistance.
